Privacy Policy

At BlackVideo, your privacy is our priority. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring transparency about how it is used.

Information We Collect

To provide personalized features, BlackVideo requires user registration. You may register using a native email/password or through third-party login services such as Google, Facebook, or GitHub. When registering, we collect basic account information such as your email address and display name. For third-party logins, we may also access profile details permitted by those services (e.g., profile image, name).

How We Use Your Information

- To manage your account and authentication
- To personalize your experience and retain your settings
- To support features such as syncing, stats tracking, and optional cloud-based preferences

Third-Party Services

BlackVideo integrates certain third-party services (such as APIs for language translation, scripture data, video stats, etc.) that may temporarily access information necessary for functionality. These third parties handle data according to their own privacy policies. We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers. Ads within BlackVideo are limited, controlled, and designed to avoid disrupting your experience.

Local & Offline Data

Your video content and playback history are stored locally on your device. BlackVideo does not upload or analyze your local files.

Your Choices

- You may delete your account and data at any time
- You can choose to opt out of optional third-party services (where available)
- You have full control over app settings and can limit ad display
